About

自己紹介

I'm an Anlagenführer at Volkswagen Bratislava — operating KUKA robots and running assembly lines on rotating shifts. For nearly five years, the factory floor has been my classroom in discipline, systems-thinking, and precision under pressure.

In parallel, I've spent 3+ years self-teaching web development: from HTML/CSS fundamentals through JavaScript to full-stack React and Node.js applications. I study during morning commutes, code between shifts, and mentally architect during physical labor.

I believe in systems over motivation. My path from a factory operator to a developer isn't a leap — it's a deliberate, disciplined build. Every project I ship is a step toward my goal: creating tools and platforms that genuinely improve people's lives.

Outside of code, I train Kyokushin karate and work toward continuous self-improvement across physical, cognitive, and technical dimensions. I also completed Harvard's CS50 for deeper computer science foundations.
